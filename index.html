Production chain

Cultivation

farmer

Tea is grown on plantations, usually in mountainous regions with tropical and subtropical climates. The type of soil, altitude and climatic conditions affect the quality of the leaves.

Classification and Selection

quality

The dried leaves are sorted by size and quality, separated into batches of different categories. Packaging, the tea is packed in packets or in bulk, ready for distribution.

Distribution

middle

The packages are sent to distributors, wholesalers and retailers. Commercialization: consumers buy the tea in specialized stores, supermarkets or online.
